# Security Policy

DeadDrop is an early prototype and has not been audited.

## Reporting issues

Please report security issues privately. If GitHub private advisories are available for this repository, use that path. Otherwise, contact the repository owner through their preferred private channel.

Helpful details include:

- affected commit or version;
- device and platform;
- reproduction steps;
- impact on message confidentiality, integrity, local storage, or packet handling.

## In scope

- Android local storage and key handling
- group invite protection
- message encryption and authentication
- signed identity handling
- packet parsing
- accidental persistence of audio or secrets
- Android permissions

## Out of scope

DeadDrop does not claim to protect against jamming, traffic analysis, compromised devices, malicious group members, or legal restrictions on radio use.
